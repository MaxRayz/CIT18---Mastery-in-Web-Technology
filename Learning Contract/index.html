LEARNING CONTRACT

Expectations:

I expect to learn and have a better understanding on topics that are to be taught in this course subject. I do hope that the instructor’s teaching style adapts to the class’s learning style. I expect to have a fun and engaging time learning, while also seeing that everyone will be treated fairly. I also expect that the things I am taught in this subject will help me in my future career path.

Contributions:

I will do my part and ensure that I have a perfect attendance, so long as I can help it. I will submit all activities given to me on time, while doing trying to actively participate in recitations and any other activities that require it. I will not cause problems for my instructors and classmates by being a nuisance. I will do my best to get high scores on activities by listening to the lectures and studying what I do not fully understand.

Motivations:

Knowing how much my tuition costs is big enough of a motivation. The support of my family keeps me going. My curiosity helps keep me engaged, and knowing I need to pass this subject if I want to be one step closer to graduating does help a lot in trying to achieve my goals. I know that this subject will further broaden my understanding on technology, and that keeps me motivated.

Hindrances:

If there are online activities and lessons that are to be done at home, my internet connection may sometimes cause me problems. Frequent blackouts/power interruptions may be a hindrance for online lessons and activities to be done at home. My mental health is not always the best, and that can can cost me losing what is being taught.
